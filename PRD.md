# Weather Widget — PRD

## What it is
A single-page weather widget that shows the current temperature, time, and location of whoever visits the URL, with a moody illustration that matches the actual weather conditions there.

## Who it's for
Anyone who opens the URL — no login, no setup.

## What it does
- Auto-detects the visitor's location from the browser
- Fetches current weather + local time from Open-Meteo
- Displays temperature, city + country, "Today" + local time
- Shows a background illustration that matches the current condition (clear / cloudy / rain / snow / fog) and time of day (day / night)
- Matches the Lofoten reference style: layered silhouettes, soft atmosphere, white text

## What it does not do
- No forecast (just right now)
- No multiple cities, no search, no settings
- No login, no saved preferences
- No hourly/daily breakdown

## Success
A visitor opens it and within a second sees their city, the real temperature, and a scene that feels like the actual weather outside their window.
